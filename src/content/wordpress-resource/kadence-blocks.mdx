## Key Positioning

Kadence Blocks represents the sweet spot for teams needing **visual editing sophistication** with **ready-made professional templates**. While Greenshift and Greenlight offer superior technical architecture, Kadence provides the template library and visual controls that can get projects from concept to launch in hours, not days.

**Perfect for:** Agencies needing quick turnaround, teams with mixed technical skills, projects where speed to market trumps optimal performance.

**Consider alternatives when:** Building design systems from scratch, prioritizing maximum performance, or working on projects requiring extensive customization.
