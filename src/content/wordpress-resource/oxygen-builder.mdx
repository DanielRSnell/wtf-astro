## Key Positioning

Oxygen Builder represents **code-level visual development** for WordPress with complete architectural control and maximum performance. While traditional builders offer simplicity, Oxygen provides the power and flexibility needed for advanced developers building custom, high-performance sites.

**Perfect for:** Advanced developers and agencies, teams requiring complete design control, performance-critical projects, custom application development, professionals comfortable with HTML/CSS, sites needing complex dynamic functionality, developers wanting visual workflow efficiency.

**Consider alternatives when:** You need beginner-friendly interfaces, lack HTML/CSS knowledge, require extensive pre-built templates, prefer traditional WordPress theming, need simple drag-and-drop functionality, or operate basic content sites without custom requirements.

## The Developer Control Advantage

Oxygen Builder's strength lies in providing complete architectural control over WordPress sites through visual development tools, enabling advanced developers to achieve maximum performance and customization while maintaining development workflow efficiency and eliminating theme limitations.
